About ScuPlan

ScuPlan is a comprehensive diving planning application designed to help divers plan safe and enjoyable dives. Whether you're a recreational diver or technical diver, ScuPlan provides the tools you need.

Features:
  • Dive plan calculations
  • Gas consumption analysis
  • Technical diving calculations
  • AI-powered diving assistant
  • Metric/Imperial unit conversion
  • Offline functionality
Safety Notice:

Always dive within your certification limits and use proper dive computers. This tool is for planning assistance only.
