About Me
About the Developer

ScuPlan was created by a passionate scuba diving enthusiast and software developer who recognized the need for comprehensive, free diving planning tools accessible to divers worldwide.

Project Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and completely free diving planning tools to the global diving community. We believe that safety-critical diving calculations should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

Key Features
  • 100% Free: No hidden costs, premium features, or subscription fees
  • Offline Capability: Works without internet connection once loaded
  • Technical Diving Support: Advanced calculations for technical divers
  • Educational Resources: Comprehensive dive theory and safety information
  • World Dive Sites: Database of famous diving locations worldwide
  • AI Assistant: Zero-cost intelligent diving guidance
Safety Notice

Important: ScuPlan is designed as a planning aid and educational tool. Always dive within your certification limits and use proper dive computers during actual dives. This tool should supplement, not replace, proper diving training and equipment.
